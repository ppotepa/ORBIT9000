WHAT IS CROSS-EXCHANGE ARBITRAGE?
=================================

Cross-exchange arbitrage is a trading strategy that makes money from price 
differences between exchanges. Here's how it works:

• BUY a token on Exchange A (where price is lower)
• SELL the same token on Exchange B (where price is higher)  
• POCKET the difference as profit

Example:
--------
BONK Token Price Check:
• Raydium DEX:     $0.000034 (cheaper)
• Binance CEX:     $0.000036 (more expensive)
• Price Gap:       $0.000002 per token
• Percentage Gain: 5.88% before fees

WHY THIS STRATEGY WORKS ON SOLANA:
==================================

1. FAST TRANSACTIONS: Solana processes trades in seconds, not minutes
2. LOW FEES: Network fees are typically under $0.01 per transaction
3. MARKET INEFFICIENCIES: Prices don't sync instantly between exchanges
4. HIGH VOLATILITY: Meme tokens create larger price gaps

The key is SPEED - price gaps close quickly as other traders spot them too.

STEP-BY-STEP TRADING PROCESS:
============================

1. SETUP PHASE:
   • Create accounts on multiple exchanges
   • Set up Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare)
   • Complete KYC verification
   • Fund accounts with base currency (USDC, USDT)

2. MONITORING PHASE:
   • Watch prices across all exchanges
   • Use CoinGecko or DexScreener for quick comparison
   • Set up price alerts for your target tokens
   • Check every 2-5 minutes during active hours

3. IDENTIFY OPPORTUNITY:
   • Find price difference between exchanges
   • Calculate percentage gap: (High Price - Low Price) / Low Price × 100%
   • Verify sufficient liquidity on both sides
   • Ensure gap is large enough to cover all fees

4. EXECUTE TRADES:
   • Buy on cheaper exchange immediately
   • Transfer token to expensive exchange (if needed)
   • Sell on expensive exchange
   • Complete entire cycle as fast as possible

5. REBALANCE:
   • Convert profits to stable currency
   • Redistribute funds for next opportunity
   • Keep balanced amounts on both DEX and CEX sides

ALGORITHMIC GUIDELINES (BEGINNER VERSION)
=========================================

This section gives you a simple, repeatable process for cross-exchange arbitrage. Follow these steps every time you trade:

1. CHECK PRICES
---------------
Look up your target token price on at least two exchanges (one DEX, one CEX).

2. FIND A PRICE GAP
-------------------
Is the price on Exchange A at least 0.5% lower than on Exchange B (after fees)? If yes, you have an opportunity!

3. CHECK LIQUIDITY
------------------
Make sure you can buy and sell the amount you want without moving the price too much. Check order book depth or recent trade volume.

4. CALCULATE FEES
-----------------
Add up trading fees, transfer fees, and network fees. Make sure your profit is bigger than all fees combined.

5. EXECUTE QUICKLY
------------------
Buy the token on the cheaper exchange. If needed, transfer it to the expensive exchange. Sell it there as soon as possible.

6. RECORD YOUR TRADE
--------------------
Write down your buy price, sell price, fees, and net profit. This helps you learn and improve.

7. REPEAT & IMPROVE
-------------------
Look for new opportunities. Adjust your process if you notice mistakes or delays. Try to get faster and more accurate each time.

CALCULATING PROFIT:
==================

FORMULA:
Net Profit = (Sell Price - Buy Price) × Quantity - Total Fees

FEES TO CONSIDER:
• Trading fees: 0.1-0.25% per trade
• Transfer fees: $1-10 depending on exchange
• Network fees: ~$0.01 on Solana
• Slippage: 0.1-1% on low liquidity tokens

EXAMPLE CALCULATION:
Trade Size: $1,000
Price Gap: 3%
Gross Profit: $30
Trading Fees: $4 (0.4%)
Transfer Fee: $5
Net Profit: $21 (2.1%)

RISK MANAGEMENT:
===============

MAJOR RISKS:
• Price movement during execution
• Transfer delays between exchanges
• Exchange downtime or withdrawal issues
• Competition from trading bots
• Insufficient liquidity

MITIGATION STRATEGIES:
• Act fast - complete trades within minutes
• Use market orders for speed when needed
• Keep funds on both sides ready
• Start with smaller amounts
• Use reputable exchanges only
• Set daily loss limits
